FACTS ABOUT NEPAL

It is a landlocked country located in South Asia.It is surrounded by China,India

Kathmandu is Nepal's capital and Nepali is its official laanguage

There are 15 prominent ethnic groups. Hinduism(81.3%),Buddhism(9%) and Islam(4.4%) are the major religions

Nepal has a federal,republic and democratic government.Bidhya Devi Bhandari and Khadga Prasad Sharm Oli are the President and Prime Minister respectively

Nepal occupies an area of 147k km2 . The population is approximately 28cr

As per 2021 estimates, Nepal's GDP is 36 billion dollars.Nepalese Rupee is the country's currency

Forest cover is approx. 40% of the total land area, with an additional 4.3% scrubland which is increasing gradually-a positive environmental sign

Dashain,Tihar,Teej , Chhath,Maghi,Sakela,Holi,Eid,Christmas and Nepali New Year are the notable festivals of the country
